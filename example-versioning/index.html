This machine learning algorithm uses a convolutional neural network to predict the image uploaded above. The neural network is trained on a dataset of thousands of images and can recognize a variety of objects and patterns.

When you click the "Predict" button, the image will be sent to the neural network and a prediction will be made. The predicted label will be displayed below the image.
